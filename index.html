About Us

Prajyukttam is the Technical Festival of School of Technology, Assam Don Bosco University. Prajyukttam grows each year and now has become a mega inter-college event. This year the festival kicks off at 17th October. A 3-day festival fueled by the excitement and energy of students from and outside the Institute. Students battling for the top with all their technical prowess. Explore your own limits, Innovate with your ingenious minds and Inspire others to go beyond.
