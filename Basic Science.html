About Us

To arouse and cultivate students’ interest in learning basic science. To assist students in carrying out self-directed activities in areas of interest in science. To encourage students to participate actively in the activities organized by the club during Prajyukttam. To enable students to gain organisational experience by participating and volunteering in events organized by the club during the academic year.

EVENT 1

Paper Presentation

  • Register

  • Description

    Event will test participants on three grounds: web design, web scripting and back-end knowledge. Each of the three tests will have a weightage depending on the difficulty level of the test. Hence the event will favour people who have knowledge on all 3 disciplines rather than a participant who is proficient on only one or two of the challenges. For web design, participants will be given an objective to accomplish using HTML and CSS with the freedom of using their own creativity. Participants will be judged for their proficiency with the languages involved, as well as User Experience and usability of the overall result. For web scripting, participants will be given objectives to complete in a given web page using JavaScript. Participants will be judged on whether they accomplish the objectives in the expected way or not. For testing Back-end knowledge, participants will be tested on their knowledge of MySQL and PHP.

  • Rules and Regulations

    1. The paper must be documented in the standard IEEE format.
    2. The maximum Number of participants per presentation is restricted to 2. Anyone willing to present it singly may do so.
    3. The presentation must be in the powerpoint format.
    4. The time limit per presentation is 8 minutes, followed by a 2 minute Q and A session.
    5. One hard copy of the paper must be submitted prior to the presentation.
    6. Eligibility Criteria : The participant must be a student pursuing undergraduate or post graduate course in any recognized college/University/Institute. It is a mandate for the participants to carry their ID cards provided by their respective institutes at the time of the registration.

  • Registration Fee:

    Rs 50/person

    Event Date:

    24th October 2019

EVENT 2

Retrace your Basics

  • Register

  • Description

    Level 1 – “Brain Teaser”

    The participants are supposed to solve maximum number of questions involving basic science, maths and quantitative aptitude from a set of 30, in a period of 5 minutes.

    Level 2 – “Tape a tale”

    Description : Extempore speech from a pool of basic scientific processes or principles. Time Limit – 3 minutes per speaker.

  • Rules and Regulations

    1. The participant scoring maximum marks in total will be the winner of the event (Cummulative evaluation)
    2. The participant must be a student of Assam Don Bosco University.

  • Registration Fee:

    Rs 20/person

    Event Date:

    17th October 2019
